<h3><br />👋 Hii, I am</h3>

## Lakshmanshankar

I started coding during the 2020 lockdown, first with Python and later moving into web development. Switching to Linux in 2022 shaped a lot of my workflow and interests — customizing my setup, building dotfiles, and falling in love with dark themes and gradient-heavy aesthetics.

Over the time at Codevantage, I've worked on a variety of projects, including **Bullet**, a platform that helps users create beautiful websites while keeping the contents in Notion. I also contributed to **MindMap**, a tool that visualizes Notion pages as mind maps.

During this phase, I discovered a deep interest in rich text editors. That exploration led me to contribute to **Lexical** and eventually build my own editor, **Formless**.

I currently work as a **Software Engineer** at [Bounteous](https://bounteous.com/), where I work on the frontend of a large-scale analytics platform.

Apart from programming, I enjoy cycling — especially on the weekends.

I maintain a list of tools I use regularly for development. [Check it out here!](https://lakshmanshankar.github.io/blog/what-i-use-in-2025)
